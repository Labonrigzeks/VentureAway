Summer vacation: Monte Carlo, Monaco

If you want to see casinos and expensive cars, the little city-state of Monaco is a great place to visit during the summer. Situated in the southeast of France, the city is known for car races and lots of money. But you don't have to be rich to travel there! We can highly recommend the area Monte Carlo, which is the beautiful hill over the casino area.

Check out TripAdvisor's page for Monaco, or visitmonaco.com for more info.
