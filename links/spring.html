Spring Vacations

Where do you want to go?

Spring is a wonderful season, with the flowers blooming and the trees and plants growing back again after the winter break. We can among others recommend a trip to Japan, especially in spring.

Or maybe you want to select another season?

Spring vacation: Kyoto, Japan

Do you think the spring in Japan is beautiful? We do! The flowers of the cherry tree blooms to its fullest in the wonderful Japanese environments, and together with the temples it really shines. The city of Kyoto, west of Tokyo, has over 1600 temples for you to visit, so it's up to you to get started!

We can help you along the way too. Inside Kyoto is a travel guide website, check out their section for the best temples of Kyoto. You can also take a look at Trip Advisor's page for Kyoto.
