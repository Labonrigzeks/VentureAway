Winter Vacations

Where do you want to go?

Do you want to see the northern lights, which are mostly available in winter from Norway and Iceland? Maybe you just want to visit a southern beach when the sun isn't as burning as in summer? We have some tips for traveling in winter, come see our favourite places!

Or maybe you want to select another season?

Winter vacation: Senja, Norway

Senja is an island in northern Norway and has beautiful varied environments. In winter the northern lights can be a common sight, but we recommend you check out NorthernLight's calendar for Senja beforehand.

You can check out VisitSenja.no (available in English) for more information about traveling there, and also check out TripAdvisor's page for Senja.
