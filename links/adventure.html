Take The First Step Towards Adventure...

Read on to learn about our various destinations

Start the journey towards distant lands in the near future. Travel, and experience the world as its meant to be seen!

Take a Journey to Peru

Do you wish it could be summer everyday? Do you hope for blue skies and warm weather in the morning? Do you want to witness the huge beauty of the Nazca lines? Perhaps visit Machu Picchu? Let the adventure begin today!

Come and see the land of the Incas: Peru!

Take a Journey to Norway

From the high tops of their mountains, to their great blue fjords. Norway ties together the old and the new. Go see viking settlements. Go see the new polished architecture of the cities. See the history that developed here. See the changes made, and learn about the battles fought.

Come and see the equanimous landscape of Norway!

Take a Journey to Japan

Perhaps you'd like to focus on the old? See the result of long kept traditions and honour. Perhaps you wish to view nature, be one of the few to witness the sakura tree blossom? From ancient temples to new-age arcade houses, Japan is a given addition to any adventurers bucket-list.

Come and see the continuous ways of Japan!

Take a Journey to New York

If empty and quiet places aren't for you, feel free to pump up the noise with the busy streets of New York. Commonly known but (these days) rarely refered to as The Big Apple, New York homes many exlorers of urban wonderlands. If this is a step you've thought about making then dont wait! Newyork is waiting for you!

Come and see the ticking of the perpetual city: New York!

Take a Journey to Scotland

Ever wanted to see the Hogwarts express? Stay a few night in Fort William, then when you feel ready take a trip to Glenfinnan and see the iconic bridge where Harry was first attacked by dementors! Some less fantasy but just as magic: explore the highland castles, see what the parts of history that are left on our planet!

Come and see the proud culture of Scotland!
